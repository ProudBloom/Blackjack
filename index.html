Blackjack

Press HIT button to play a card.
Each card has it's own value matching that of a card.
J,Q,K are worth 10 points each.
A is worth either 1 point or 11 points depending on your score.
Press STAND button when you feel like it (don't exceed 21 points or you will bust!).
After pressing this button, the AI will play.
The closest score to the value of 21 points wins!
Press the DEAL button to start a new round and clear the table.
Enjoy!
